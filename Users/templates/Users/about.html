Welcome to GoLift Weightlifting Club!

This app will allow you to effectively log your training, view your training and predict how likely you are to become a development, Commonwealth or Olympic lifter. These predictions will be based on historical British Weightlifing data from 2015 to 2019. The factors taken into consideration for this prediciton are your Total (Snatch + Clean&Jerk), Back Squat and Front Squat. The model will take your best Snatch and Clean&Jerk on any given day from your training logs as your Total. Good luck and enjoy!

Please be aware that your prediction increases in accuracy with a greater amount of data from your training log.
